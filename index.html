Most recent work

dodgeTH!S

A game about death, a loving pet and dodging. dodgeTH!S is a challenging mobile 3D Top-Down RPG which presents the story of a young mage, Kazimir, who overestimated his abilities and fell victim to his pride and is now trapped in the underworld. We follow him and his dog (who courageously follows him everywhere) in Kazimirs quest full of twist to redeem his life back.

The game has a unique ability set. Kazimir has no posibility to attack his foes as long as he doesn't fill energy from somewhere. You will be able to only dodge and reflect enemy attacks untill you collect energy, when you will be able to start shooting.

Throught the game you will be required to change your strategy and listen to your dog to advance and inspect the environment to learn about the lore.

Demo

Neighbours From Hell 3D

A reinterpretation of the classic game with a sprinkle of music. The game has the same mechanics as the classic game.

You can hide and do specific pranks to your neighbour but you can also dance. You have a playlist of songs you can modify that you can listen and dance to.

The neighbour has a anger meter, stops and shouts when he discovers a prank and will fall when the meter is full.

As I mentioned above, it is basically the same game in 3D but with no good art and no license.

Demo Repo

Species Mutation Algorithm

A simulation of species coping to new environments. Species Mutation Algorithm is an testing environment for a genetic algorithm which aims to be a part of a larger MMORPG.

It should take care of movement from biom to biom limitation, gradually adaption to new environmental aspects and evaluation of new born species. The system also takes into account different equipment a player has equiped to its avatar.

Repo

Wishing for a Meddow

Wishing for a Meddow is a procedurally generated game about dreams. Not ordinary dreams but the surreal ones.

It is the first game I developed, I was inspired by The Witness, the almost inexistent guidance it gave you and the tense athmosphere it created. Thus I was determined to create a similar feeling, but based on Romanian tales. Another inpiration for me was the classic Prince of Persia, preciselly, the fixed time within you had to finish the game. It is also a mechanic in my project.

Wishing for a Meddow will require you to solve non-linear puzzles to discover a way to wake up.

Repo

Animal Oasis

Animal Oasis is a VR experience designed for everybody, implemented in Unity, unsing Oculus Quest 2. In this game you will be able to interact with varied animal species and observe them in diverse situations over different seasons. The purpose of this game is for you to relax in a frinedly world and pet whoever you want, be it a cat or a tiger.

Forgotten Tales

Forgotten Tales is an voxelized MMORPG. It features different classes with diverse abilities.

It is the work of 20+ passionate developers and the project has been done in about a month. It is nor a finished, nor a polished project, but it is a prototype that gave each of us the oportunity to know each other and grow our skills. Thus it is special.
